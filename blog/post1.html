The Best Window Cleaning Tips

Keeping your windows clean can be a daunting task, but with the right tips, you can achieve sparkling results. Here are some expert tips:

  • Use a squeegee for a streak-free finish.
  • Clean windows on a cloudy day to prevent streaks from the sun.
  • Use a mixture of vinegar and water for an eco-friendly cleaning solution.
